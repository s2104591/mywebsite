Roman Colosseum."Originally completed eighty years after the birth of Christ, the Colosseum was the
largest of the amphitheatres constructed by the Roman Empire, at one time regularly hosting 50,000
spectators for the sort of brutal entertainment that was depicted in the film 'Gladiator'."
