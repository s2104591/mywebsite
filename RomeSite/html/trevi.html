Trevi Fountain

Trevi. "This is the largest Baroque period fountain in the whole of Rome and a timeless masterpiece that
was designed by Nicola Salvi and Giuseppe Pannini. The Trevi Fountain takes its name from its positioning
at the junction of three roads. At night, the fountain is bathed in golden light, and is simply spectacular."
